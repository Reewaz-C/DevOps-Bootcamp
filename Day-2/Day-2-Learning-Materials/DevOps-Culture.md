# DEVOPS CULTURE

A Devops culture is a close collaboration and shared resopsibilities between the devlopment and the operations teams for efficient deployment and maintainance of quality software.

[Devops Culture](../Img/DevOps_Culture.jpg)

## Key Aspects of DevOps Culture

1. Shared responsibility:

    - Development and Operations teams share the responsibility for sucess or failure of a project

    - Developers not only create the software but also observer the entire lifecycle.

    - Shared ownership leads to better understanding of the challanges.

2. Transparent communication:

    - Teams collaborate closely breaking tradition ways.
    
    - Development teams gaines user requirements and Operations teams recives customer feedbacks.

3. Continious Learning:

    - Devops encourages continious learning and improvements.

    - Helps to stay updated on new industry trends and technologies.

4. Feedback and Improving:

    - Helps in identifying areas for improvements.

    - Iterate and adapt based on feedback from the stake holders.

## Benefits of Devops Culture

1. The most impactful benefit of adopting Devops culture is frequent high quality software deployments.

2. Devops culture promotes high level of transperancy and collaboration resulting in quality decision making and even higher job satisfaction.

3. Devops culture fosters shared responsibilities between Development and Operations teams for sucess or failure of a project.

## Challanges of Devops Culture

1. Embracing Devops dulture require teams to make significant changes to how they work.

2. If there is a history of conflict between the teams or individuals then the cultivation of trust and transparancy would be difficult.

3. If the benefits of change in culture is not clearly lined-out it can be difficult to drive acceptance and willingness to put into the implementation.
